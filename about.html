About

My Story

Background

I currently live in Singapore but I am originally from Hong Kong and have also lived in the United Kingdom for 6 years. I studied law and worked as a corporate lawyer in Singapore and London, but ever since high school I have always had an innate passion for the intersection of design and technology, and signed up to graphic design and 3D CAD subjects in high school, summer school and related extracurricular activities whenever possible throughout school and university. I discovered UX design in 2019 and believe that becoming an interaction designer would be the ideal job for me due to the combination of design thinking, psychology and empathy, and have since taken multiple courses to gain experience and transition into the field.
